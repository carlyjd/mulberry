Program Details

  • Children must be turning 3 by August 31, 2025
  • Children must be potty trained
  • Schedule: Monday through Thursday, 8:30 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Daily: Bring a change of clothes and shoes suitable for outdoor play

Each day includes music, movement, outdoor play, stories, and the introduction of letters, numbers, and exploring how the world works. We will make full use of the beautiful home space to sing, dance, explore, imagine, create—and most of all, PLAY!
