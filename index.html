Welcome to Around the Mulberry Tree Preschool!

I am excited to offer a home-based preschool with emphasis on sensory learning and outdoor play. I am a wife, a mom of teenagers, and a former gifted education teacher. I love to plan fun activities for children such as taking a picture book as a jumping point for play or creation, making up songs that fit what we are learning about, and giving them hands-on experiences that build their confidence and understanding of the world. I need some children who can do these activities that I enjoy planning!

I believe children learn best when they feel challenged, supported, and seen for their individual strengths and interests. I approach the classroom climate from the Responsive Classroom methodology which is child centered and empowers students to make positive choices through teaching social-emotional skills, clear expectations, routines and providing a safe and engaging environment.
